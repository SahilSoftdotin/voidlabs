AURORA — Music Platform

Crafting an intuitive digital experience for music discovery and streaming

The Challenge

AURORA needed a next-generation streaming interface that made music discovery effortless. The platform had to balance powerful features with simplicity, encouraging users to spend more time exploring and enjoying music.

Our Solution

We designed an elegant, gesture-based interface with immersive album artwork visualization, intelligent playlist recommendations, and smooth micro-interactions that made every action feel natural and delightful.

Scope

  • ✓ User Research & Insights
  • ✓ Wireframing & Prototyping
  • ✓ UI Design & Interaction
  • ✓ Motion Design & Animation
  • ✓ Developer Handoff

Results

The new interface resulted in a 58% increase in daily active users and 2.3x improvement in playlist sharing. User engagement time increased by 43%, demonstrating the power of thoughtful design paired with powerful technology.
